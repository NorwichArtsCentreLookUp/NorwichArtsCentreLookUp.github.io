Submitting Content to the Digital Map.

We welcome submissions to the Look Up Trail! Please follow the instructions below and we will add your entry.

To submit photos email
photos@lookuptrail.co.uk
Make sure to put:
title in the email subject line
description and address in the main email
and remember to attach your photo!

To submit sounds email
sounds@lookuptrail.co.uk
Make sure to put:
title in the email subject line
description and address in the main email
and remember to attach your sound file!

Launch Map

All entries must be checked before being added to the map. This is done by a real person is can take several days.
